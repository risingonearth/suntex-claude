About Suntex Corporation

Suntex Corporation, based in Delhi, India, is a leading all-India distributor of high-quality textile machinery parts. Founded by Late Sh. Ram Chand Gaba, we specialize in supplying critical components like spindles, pulleys, gears, bearings, and belts to required customers nationwide.

With a focus on reliability and precision, we ensure seamless operations for our clients by delivering durable, industry-standard parts. Our mission is to empower the textile industry with efficient solutions, backed by exceptional customer service and timely delivery.
